# Support

## Ways to get help

1.  **Issue Tracker:** Please open an issue in the [GitHub Issue Tracker](https://github.com/ArshVermaGit/nexus-ai-life-os/issues) for bugs and feature requests.
2.  **Discussions:** Join the [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/ArshVermaGit/nexus-ai-life-os/discussions) (if enabled) to ask questions and share ideas.

## Support Policy

We try to respond to issues within a few days. NEXUS is a community-driven project, so support depends on contributor availability.
